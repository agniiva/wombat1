About Us

We're a luxury home theater business with 14 years of experience designing state-of-the-art theaters and smart homes. We strive for excellence in every project, ensuring that each client gets a custom experience tailored to their needs. We use the latest tech and design trends to create exceptional home entertainment spaces, from immersive audiovisual systems to intuitive control interfaces.

Vishwas Maney founded Home is Theater in 2009 out of his immense adoration for music and unwavering enthusiasm for cinema.
He is intricately involved in the design and calibration of every project undertaken by the company, which results in a truly bespoke home theater room.
